Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. Your data is stored in a private, encrypted Supabase instance with Row-Level Security, meaning only you can access your data. We also offer a local-only processing option for maximum privacy.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You have complete control. You can delete individual sessions or your entire account and all associated data with a single click from your account settings.

What file formats are supported?

MindVault currently supports `.txt`, `.md`, `.xlsx`, and `.zip` files (for ChatGPT exports). You can also paste text directly. We are continuously expanding our parsing capabilities.

What is the Opportunity Score?

It's our proprietary algorithm that analyzes every idea against four key metrics: Revenue Potential (R), Market Urgency (U), Competitive Moat (M), and Ease of Effort (E). It's designed to help you focus on what will make you money the fastest.
